About US



Created for a capstone project, out of a growing need to have an online library for students.

MetroState Library system was founded in October of 2022, with a goal to create an online plateform for a college library system.


Many individuals find themselves in need of a book, without the hassle of permanent ownership. It is no secret that books can be an expensive purchase for individuals, who likely will not be keeping the books long term. The library promises to alleviate this burden by providing individuals with the ability to rent the books for a short period of time.However the cost of inventory maintenance puts added stress on the library. It is difficult to maintain inventory and offer patrons the ability to browse books without the add expense that comes with the overhead. We want to compete with companies, by providing an online service for individuals to browse books, rent the books needed, purchase books, and pay any fees accrued. This service will allow the library the ability to manage their book inventory, rent and sell books, and collect any fees.
